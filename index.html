Miss Prince

By Hey Steve

About the Song

Miss Prince by Hey Steve (aka Republican Hair) is a heartfelt indie-rock tribute to the Legendary musician Prince. This song showcases a blend of admiration for his artistic impact with themes of nostalgia, stardom, and loss. A love letter to Prince's artistry and influence, Miss Prince references his iconic style, biggest hits, and the profound void left by his passing.

Key Information:

  • Album: The Prince & The Duke
  • Release Date: September 8, 2017
  • Writers: Luke Dick, Natalie Hemby, Trent Dabbs
  • Producer: Luke Dick
  • Genre: Indie Rock / Alternative POP-Rock
